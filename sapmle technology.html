new world technolgies

The role and impact of technology in both our personal and working lives is ever growing. Understanding
how people shape technology and how technology shapes people's interactions with each other and the natural world
is important - not only for those who research, develop and implement new technologies, but also for all those people
and organisations that have to use those technologies in their working and personal lives. Technology is not a neutral word.
Different people will give it different meaning depending on their viewpoint and context.
